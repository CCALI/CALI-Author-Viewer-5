CALI Lessons Next Button

The "Next" button is used to navigate through lessons. It is designed to replace the old "Back" and "Next" methods of navigation. Now students will only press the new "Next" button to continue. If they wish to navigation elsewere in the lesson they will use the slider menu for the table of conents. (see menus/slider menu & navtree)
